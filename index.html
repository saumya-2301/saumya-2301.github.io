About Me

I am a passionate and results-driven Data Scientist with a strong foundation in machine learning, data analysis, and predictive modeling. Over the years, I have honed my ability to derive actionable insights from large datasets, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions. With expertise in Python, R, and SQL, alongside hands-on experience with advanced tools such as TensorFlow, Scikit-Learn, and SQL, I excel at developing innovative solutions for complex problems. My technical toolkit also includes expertise in Tableau, MS Excel, and cloud services like Amazon AWS and Google Firebase, which I leverage to build scalable applications and data pipelines. Throughout my career, I have worked on various impactful projects, including a Stock Market Prediction System using Random Forest, and a Health Management Application powered by machine learning models for hypertension and heart attack prediction. These experiences have solidified my ability to integrate AI into real-world applications, significantly improving operational efficiency and user engagement. In addition, I have developed a feature extraction model using Tesseract OCR and OpenCV, demonstrating my ability to work with computer vision technologies. As someone who is continuously seeking to learn and grow, I have pursued certifications in data science, machine learning, and cloud computing to enhance my expertise. My goal is to leverage my skills and experience to drive innovation, contribute to impactful projects, and help organizations achieve strategic objectives through the power of data and AI.
